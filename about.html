HHF GLOBAL SUMMIT
2022 IS HERE!!

Home of Happiness Foundation Summit 2022 is proud to announce another thrilling global summit where we bring about solutions related to underpriviledged children who do not have a shelter, guardians and other day to day needs. There are over 130 million orphans in the world, you as a well wisher can help in reducing this by being one of our partners giving a home to a child. Your help will be abundantly appreciated, come and learn more on how you can help our children. Together we can bring home our children and become a family once again.
